About Indian GST:

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax in India that has replaced many indirect taxes. GST rates are categorized as 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%.

Common GST Rates:
Essential Items 0%
Basic Necessities 5%
Standard Rate 18%
Luxury Items 28%
