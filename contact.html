Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the tools?

Most tools are straightforward - just upload your file or enter your data, adjust settings if needed, and click the process button. Results are generated instantly in your browser.

Are my files secure?

Yes! All processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to download or share the results. We don't store or access your data.

What file formats are supported?

Image tools support JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP. Text tools work with plain text, JSON, CSV, and other common formats. Check each tool's page for specific format requirements.

Why isn't a tool working?

Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or checking if your file meets the size and format requirements.

Can I suggest new tools?

Absolutely! We love hearing ideas for new tools. Send us your suggestions through the contact form or email us directly. We consider all requests and prioritize based on user demand and feasibility.

Is there a mobile app?

Our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. We don't currently have dedicated mobile apps, but the web version provides the same functionality on all devices.
