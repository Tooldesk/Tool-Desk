How to Calculate Average:

The average (mean) is calculated by adding all numbers together and then dividing by the count of numbers.

Average = (Sum of all numbers) / (Total count of numbers)

Example: Average of [4, 8, 6] = (4 + 8 + 6) / 3 = 18 / 3 = 6
