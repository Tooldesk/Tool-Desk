Google Cache Best Practices

  • Regular updates: Google typically caches pages every few days to weeks
  • Indexing issues: If your page isn't cached, it might not be indexed
  • Content verification: Use cached versions to see how Google views your content
  • Mobile-first: Google caches the mobile version of your site first
  • Request indexing: Use Google Search Console to request faster caching
