UTM Best Practices

  • Be consistent: Use the same naming conventions across all campaigns
  • Use lowercase: Avoid uppercase letters to prevent duplicate tracking
  • Avoid special characters: Stick to letters, numbers, underscores, and hyphens
  • Test your links: Always verify your UTM parameters before launching campaigns
  • Document your structure: Keep a record of your UTM naming conventions
