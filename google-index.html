Indexing Best Practices

  • Robots.txt: Ensure your robots.txt isn't blocking important pages
  • Meta Tags: Avoid noindex tags on pages you want indexed
  • Sitemap: Submit a sitemap to Google Search Console
  • Internal Links: Make sure important pages are linked from your site
  • Page Speed: Improve loading times for better crawlability
