Frequently Asked Questions

Are all tools on Tool Desk free to use?

Yes, all tools on Tool Desk are completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible digital solutions to everyone without any cost barriers.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you don't need to create an account to use most of our tools. You can access them directly without any registration. However, some advanced features might require an account in the future.

Is my data safe when using your tools?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. For most tools, your data is processed entirely in your browser and never sent to our servers. For tools that require server processing, we delete all data immediately after processing.

Can I suggest a new tool to be added?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our tool collection. Please use our Contact Us page to suggest new tools, and we'll consider adding them in future updates.

How often are new tools added to the website?

We regularly update our tool collection based on user feedback and emerging digital needs. We typically add new tools every month to ensure we're providing the most relevant and useful resources.
