Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At MultiTools, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

MultiTools is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. All tool processing happens locally in your browser.

Anonymous Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to help us improve our services. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and includes information such as:

  • Most popular tools
  • General usage patterns
  • Browser and device types (anonymized)
  • Performance metrics

Contact Information

If you contact us through our contact form or email, we may collect and store the information you provide, including your name, email address, and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

Service Improvement

We use anonymous usage data to understand how our tools are being used and to improve their functionality and user experience.

Communication

If you contact us, we use your provided information to respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

Legal Compliance

We may use your information to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights and the rights of our users.

Data Security

Client-Side Processing

All tool processing happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to download or share results.

No Data Transmission

We do not transmit your files, text, or other data to our servers. Everything is processed locally on your device.

Secure Communication

When you contact us, we use secure communication channels to protect any information you share with us.

Cookies and Local Storage

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your preferences, such as your theme selection (dark/light mode). These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.

Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your theme preference and other settings. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics that would compromise your privacy.

Third-Party Services

Font Services

We use Google Fonts to provide typography for our website. Google may collect some information about your use of their services. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

No Other Third Parties

We do not share your information with any other third-party services, advertisers, or data brokers.

Your Rights

Data Control

Since we don't collect personal data, you have complete control over your information. You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove any stored preferences.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at Manojinfo@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
