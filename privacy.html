Welcome to Tool Desk (https://tooldesk.github.io/Tool-Desk/). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any data you interact with while using our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free online tools.

1. Data Processing in the Browser

Most tools on Tool Desk operate entirely within your web browser. This means:

  • Your input (e.g., text, images, code) is not sent to our servers.
  • All processing happens locally on your device.
  • We do not collect, store, or log any of your data from these tools.

Examples include:

  • Word & Character Counter
  • Base64 Encoder/Decoder
  • RGB to HEX Converter
  • And many other client-side tools

2. Tools That May Use Server Processing

A small number of tools may require temporary server-side processing. In such cases:

  • Your data is sent securely to our servers only for the purpose of performing the requested operation.
  • We do not store your data after processing.
  • All data is immediately and permanently deleted once the task is complete.

We will clearly indicate within any tool if it requires server processing.

3. No Account Required

You do not need to create an account to use most of our tools. As a result, we do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or IP address for standard usage.

4. Analytics and Cookies

We may use minimal, anonymized analytics (e.g., via Google Analytics or similar services) to understand general traffic patterns and improve our website. These tools:

  • Do not identify you personally.
  • May use cookies to track session data, but no sensitive information is stored.

You can disable cookies via your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our tools or practices. The updated policy will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us
