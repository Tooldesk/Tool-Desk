What is CPM?

CPM (Cost Per Mille) is a marketing term used to denote the price of 1,000 advertisement impressions on a webpage or platform.

Formula: CPM = (Total Cost / Total Impressions) × 1,000

Enter any two values to calculate the third:

  • Total Cost ($) - Your advertising budget
  • CPM ($) - Cost per thousand impressions
  • Total Impressions - Number of ad views
