Meta Tag Best Practices

  • Title Tag: Keep it between 50-60 characters for optimal display in search results
  • Meta Description: Aim for 150-160 characters to avoid truncation
  • Keywords: While less important for SEO today, include relevant terms
  • Place meta tags in the section of your HTML document
