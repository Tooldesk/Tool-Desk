URL Encoder/Decoder

Encode and decode URLs for safe transmission over the Internet

URL encoding is a process that converts characters into a format that can be sent over the Internet. On the Internet, URLs can only be sent using ASCII characters.

URLs often contain characters outside the ASCII set, so the URL must be converted into a valid ASCII format. URL encoding replaces unsafe ASCII characters with a "%" followed by two hexadecimal digits.

Encoding Examples

Original:
https://example.com/search?q=hello world
Encoded:
https://example.com/search?q=hello%20world
Original:
café & résumé.pdf
Encoded:
caf%C3%A9%20%26%20r%C3%A9sum%C3%A9.pdf
